About Us

ACT for Climate, founded by Ahmad Faiz Omari, is dedicated to tackling climate change in Afghanistan through education, research, and sustainable practices. The initiative focuses on raising awareness, strengthening resilience, and offering practical solutions to combat environmental challenges, particularly the growing issue of plastic waste. By engaging communities and promoting climate-conscious actions, ACT for Climate aims to empower individuals and foster change at a grassroots level. With a passionate team of young volunteers, the organization creates training programs, collaborates with experts, and drives innovation in environmental research. Their long-term vision is to expand their network, inspire collective action, and contribute to a global sustainable future. Through continuous efforts, ACT for Climate seeks not only to mitigate the effects of climate change but also to encourage widespread participation in environmental protection.
