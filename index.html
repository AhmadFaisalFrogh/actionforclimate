Empowring Youth For A Green Future In Afghanistan

Act for Climate is a youth-led, volunteer driven initiative based in Afghanistan. We are committed to raising awareness, building community resilience, and leading action against climate change and environmental challenges, especially plastic pollution.

Services

Community education and capacity building on climate change and disaster risk reduction
Research and studies on Climate and environmental issues
Awareness campaigns to reduce plastic usage
Supporting youth led climate action projects

We invite individuals, institutions, and organizations to join us in this journey and work together towards a more sustainable future.

About Us

Act for Climate is a youth-led, volunteer-driven initiative based in Afghanistan, dedicated to addressing the urgent challenges of climate change through community-based action, education, research, and sustainable practices. We believe that climate solutions begin at the grassroots level. Our work focuses on raising public awareness, reducing plastic waste, building community resilience, and empowering young people to take environmental leadership in their own communities.
